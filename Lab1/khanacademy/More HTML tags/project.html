Chocolate Chip Cookies

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 24 cookies
Ingredients Quantity
All-purpose flour 2 1/4 cups
Chocolate chips 2 cups

Steps:

1. Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C). Line baking sheets with parchment paper.

2. Mix the dry ingredients in one bowl, the wet ingredients in another, and combine. Fold in chocolate chips.

3. Scoop dough onto baking sheets and bake for 8-10 minutes. Cool before serving.

Spaghetti Carbonara

  • Time: 20 minutes
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Spaghetti 400g
Egg yolks 4

Steps:

1. Cook the spaghetti in boiling water until al dente. Reserve some pasta water.

2. In a bowl, whisk together egg yolks and grated cheese. Toss the hot spaghetti with the mixture, adding pasta water to create a creamy sauce.

3. Serve immediately with crispy pancetta and more cheese on top.

Source: BBC Good Food

Avocado Toast

  • Time: 10 minutes
  • Serves: 1
Ingredients Quantity
Bread 2 slices
Avocado 1

Steps:

1. Toast the bread slices until golden brown.

2. Mash the avocado and spread it over the toasted bread. Season with salt, pepper, and optional toppings like chili flakes or a poached egg.
